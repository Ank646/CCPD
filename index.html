About Us

The National Institute of Technology, Warangal (NIT Warangal), located in Telangana, India, is a premier engineering institution. Established in 1959 as the first of the 31 NITs in the country. It was initially known as the Regional Engineering College. Renowned for its strong emphasis on research and innovation, NIT Warangal offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering, science, and management disciplines. The institute is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and active student community. It consistently ranks among the top engineering colleges in India and has a strong alumni network contributing to diverse fields globally
